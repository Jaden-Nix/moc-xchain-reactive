üö® The Problem

Chainlink Is Not Everywhere

Chainlink price feeds are the industry standard. But they're not deployed on every chain. Building on a new L2 or testnet? Your smart contracts are basically blind.

Centralized Relayers Are Dangerous

The traditional workaround: some backend service reads Chainlink on Ethereum and pushes prices to your chain. That's a single point of failure. Goes down? Prices stop.

Oracle Attacks Are Real

We've seen it happen. Price manipulation, flash crash exploits, replay attacks. One wrong oracle number = protocols get liquidated.

‚ú® The Solution

Trustless Cross-Chain Mirroring

MOC takes a Chainlink feed on Sepolia and mirrors it to another chain using Reactive Contracts. No centralized relayer. Everything validated on-chain.

Real-Time Attack Detection

Every price is validated before storage. Zero prices? Rejected. Negative prices? Rejected. Flash crashes? Detected.

Full AggregatorV3 Compatibility

The destination implements the standard Chainlink interface. Apps don't need to change a single line of code.
